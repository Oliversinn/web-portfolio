Overfishing and poverty

More than 26 million people depend on artisanal fishing for their survival. Artisanal Fishing represent over 35% of world catches. Many of their practices contribute to overfishing and the extinction of species like sharks and sea turtles.

On the other hand, artisanal fishermen are sunken in poverty earning less than the minimum wage. Every week is harder and harder for them to make a living since overfishing have decreased marine stocks of fish.

Actually 33 % of marine stocks are being overfished.
