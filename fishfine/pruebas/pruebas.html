About us

More than 26 million people depend on artisanal fishing for their survival. Many of their practices contribute to overfishing and the extinction of species like sharks and sea turtles.

We connect artisanal fishermen with adventurers that seek affordable fishing experiences. This way, we contribute to the protection of the marine ecosystem by reducing overfishing and helping these fishermen earn a higher source of income.
